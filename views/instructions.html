Instructions

In this survey, you will watch 6 videos of web page loads.

After watching each video, your job is to grade the quality of the load you watched.

  • We use a 5-level grading scale: Excellent, Good, Fair, Poor, or Bad. You will grade some page loads of the same web page based on this scale.
  • You should grade the quality of each test load by judging the quality of the load.

Each video will show you a process of browsing a website.

(1) The first one is an example of a perfect load for the test loads you will watch. Please watch this in full (until the video turns grey).

  • On the following rating page, you should rate the quality of this load video as Excellent (failure to do so may lead to rejection of results).

(2) For the rest, watch each video until the you think the video is sufficiently loaded before moving on.

  • For each video, rate how you felt the web page loading quality was.

We use the results to look for trends in responses.

Please be sure to watch each load in its full length and answer every question in this survey honestly.

IMPORTANT: At any point during this survey, please do not press the back button or the refresh button of your web browser.

If you do on accident or you need to refresh, please restart the entire survey starting from this page.
